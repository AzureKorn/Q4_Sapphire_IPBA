Solution

What's its function?

Our Prototype's function is to give extra protective security measures by generating new codes for logging in your account on another device, it's like an extension to your current device's security. Its downside is that it can only be used if there's already a device with your account logged in.

Step By Step Process:

1. Go to your account and generate a code.

2. Save those codes, either taking notes or taking a picture.

3. When you want to log in on another device, use one of the codes you generated.

4. After using the code, you can now log in to your account and the code you used will be marked as used.
